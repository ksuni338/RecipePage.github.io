Recipe #1: Guacamole Toast

  • Time: 5-10 mins
  • Serves: 3 people
  Ingredients  
       Quantity          
   Avocado  
     3 (large size)    
   Tomato  
     1 (medium size)     
   Onion  
     1/2 (medium size)      
   Green Chilli  
     1     
   Cilantro  
     1 cup     
   Lemon Juice  
     3-4 tbsp     
   Salt  
     1/2 tbsp     
   Black pepper  
     1/4 tbsp     
   Bread  
     6 slices

How to prepare Guacamole on Toast?

Step 1: Get a big bowl.
Step 2: Peal avocados and mash them in the bowl.
Step 3: Finely dice 1/2 of a medum sized onion, 1 medium sized tomato, 1 green chilli, and 1 cup of cilantro.
Step 4: Put everything in the bowl and mix until well combined.
Step 5: Add 1/2 tbsp of salt, 1/4 tbsp of black pepper, and 3-4 tbsp of lemon juice.
Step 6: Mix well and the guacamole spread is ready.
Step 7: Toast the bread slices golden brown.
Step 8: Spread the guacamole on the toasts.
Step 9: Plate and serve.

Recipe #2: Focaccia Bread

  • Time: 4-5 hours
  • Serves: 4-5 people
   Ingredients    
     Quantity        
  Active Dry Yeast  
    1 packet (0.25 oz)  
  Bread flour  
    2^(3/4) cups 
  Semolina flour  
    1/4 cup 
  Rosemary  
    4 tbsp 
  Olive Oil  
    1 bottle 
  Salt   
    3/4 tbsp 
  Sea Salt  
    3/4 tbsp 
  Warm water  
    1 cup

How to bake Italian Focaccia Bread?

Step 1: Get a big bowl with warm water.
Step 2: Add 1 packet (0.25 oz) of active dry yeast, 2 tbsp of olive oil, 2 tbsp of minced rosemary, 3/4 of salt, 1/4 cup of semolina flour
Step 3: Mix well.
Step 4: Add 2(2/4) bread flour(reserve about 1/4 cup for the kneading).
Step 5: Mix in bowl until a sticky dough forms.
Step 6: Remove from the bowl and knead on a flat surface with reserved flour and 2 additional tablespoons of olive oil, for about 7-8 minutes, until you have a smooth, elastic, but slightly sticky dough.
Step 7: Put the dough back in the bowl and drizzle the top with some olive oil and let it rise until it doubbles in size.
Step 8: Take a baking tray and grase it with olive oil.
Step 9: Flatten the dough on the tray.
Step 10: Poke dough with finger tips.
Step 11: Let it rise for 45 mins or until it doubles in size.
Step 12: Brush lightly with olive oil, top with more rosemary and sea salt.
Step 13: Bake at 475 degrees F. for 14-15 minutes
Step 14: Let it cool down and serve.

Recipe #3: Pasta Marinara

  • Time: 1-2 hours
  • Serves: 4-5 people
      Ingredients     
     Quantity       
  Boiled Pasta 
    1 box (16 oz) 
  Crushed canned tomatos 
    2 cans (16 oz)  
  Freshly sliced Basil
 
    1/3 cup 
  Chopped Garlic
 
    6-8 cloves 
  Onion
 
    1/2 medium sized 
  Salt 
    1/2 tbsp 
  Sugar (optional)
 
    1 pinch per taste  
  Olive Oil
 
    3-4 tbsp 
  Bay Leave
 
    2 leaves 
  Water
 
    16 oz 
  Black Pepper
 
    1/4 tbsp

How to prepare Pasta Marinara?

Step 1: Heat 3-4 tbsp of olive oil
Step 2: Add 1/2 medium sized chopped onlion, 6-8 cloves of chopped garlic, 1/2 tbsp of salt, one pinch of sugar, and 1/4 tbsp of black pepper.
Step 3: Cook on medium high heat for 5 mins or until the onions become transparent
Step 4: Add 2 cans (16 oz) of tomatos along with 1 can of water.
Step 5: Mix Well and add 1/3 of freshly sliced basil.
Step 6: Allow sauce to simmer uncovered over medium high heat for about 30 -45 minutes until it thickens.
Step 7: Make sure the sauce is simmering but not boiling and give it a good stir in between so it doesn't stick or burn on the bottom.
Step 8: Once sauce is done remove it from the heat, taste test to see if it needs any more salt and pepper. Add if necessary.
Step 9: Add boiled pasta to the sauce.
Step 10: Mix until well combined.
Step 11: Serve hot.
